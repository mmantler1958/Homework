A Little About Chess

History of Chess

It is thought that Chess originated during the 7th century in India, and later spread to the rest of the world and over time has changed to what it is today.

Components of Chess

Chess is played on a square board on an 8x8 grid of squares, with the squares alternating in color from light to black as shown below.

Their are two sets of pieces, one for each player. Each piece in set matches the shade of the squares on the board. For example, for black and white squares, one set would be black and the other white.

The piece names from left to right are:

  • Pawn
  • King
  • Queen
  • Bishop
  • Rook
  • Knight
